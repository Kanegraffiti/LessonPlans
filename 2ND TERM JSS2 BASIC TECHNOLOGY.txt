SECOND SEMESTER LESSON PLAN – JSS2 BASIC TECHNOLOGY (WEEKS 1–8)

WEEK 1
Date: __________________
Class: JSS2 (Basic 8)
Duration: 40 minutes
Period: 1st Period
Topic: Safety in the Workshop
Subtopic: Personal and machine safety rules
Reference Materials:
- NERDC Basic Technology Curriculum
Instructional Materials:
- Safety signs chart
- PPE samples (goggles, gloves)
Previous Knowledge:
- Learners have seen school workshop tools.
Entry Behaviour:
- Learners mention risky workshop actions.
Behavioural Objectives:
- State at least five workshop safety rules.
Lesson Presentations:
- Teacher introduces PPE and warning symbols.
Lesson Content:
- Always wear PPE, keep tools properly, avoid horseplay, report faults.
Evaluation:
- Mention four workshop safety precautions.
Conclusion:
- Safety is the first rule in technology practice.
Assignment:
- Draw and label three workshop safety signs.
