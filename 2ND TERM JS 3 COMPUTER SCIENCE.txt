SECOND SEMESTER LESSON PLAN – JSS3 COMPUTER SCIENCE (WEEKS 1–8)

WEEK 1
Date: __________________
Class: JSS3 (Basic 9)
Duration: 40 minutes
Period: 1st Period
Topic: Computer Networks
Subtopic: Meaning and types of networks
Reference Materials:
- NERDC Computer Studies Curriculum
Instructional Materials:
- Network topology charts
Previous Knowledge:
- Learners know basic computer components.
Entry Behaviour:
- Learners mention places where computers are connected.
Behavioural Objectives:
- Define computer network.
- List at least three network types.
Lesson Presentations:
- Teacher introduces LAN, MAN, and WAN.
Lesson Content:
- Network is interconnection of computers for sharing resources.
Diagram:
PC1 -- Switch -- PC2
             \
              PC3
Evaluation:
- Define LAN and WAN.
Conclusion:
- Networks enable communication and resource sharing.
Assignment:
- State two advantages of a computer network.
